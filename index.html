Features

Everything you need to understand your money

No manual data entry. No bank integrations to set up. Just connect your email and let Nura do the rest.

Email-powered sync

Connect Gmail or Outlook and Nura automatically reads transaction receipts in real time. No browser extensions, no screen scraping — just secure, read-only email access.

Gmail Outlook Auto-sync

AI categorisation

Nura automatically assigns the right category to each transaction — Food, Transport, Shopping, Utilities, and more. You can correct any mistake in one tap.

Smart analytics

Category breakdowns, monthly trend charts, top merchants, and payment mode analysis — all updated automatically as new transactions arrive.

Budget alerts

Set monthly budgets per category. Nura checks every few hours and notifies you the moment you approach or exceed a limit.

PDF statement upload

Don't have email receipts? Upload a PDF bank statement and Nura will parse and import your transactions automatically.

30+ currencies

HKD, SGD, MYR, THB, IDR, PHP, VND, INR, USD and more. Set your base currency and all totals are shown accordingly.
